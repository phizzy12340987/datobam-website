How We Work

Our Investigation Workflow

A systematic, transparent, and strictly confidential 4-step approach engineered to gather rock-solid evidence safely and legally.

01

Confidential Intake

Phase 01

Every case starts with a secure and encrypted consultation to understand your requirements, establish scope, and verify legal standing without putting your privacy at risk.

  • Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) coverage
  • Anonymous/Pseudonym intake option
  • Initial feasibility & legal review
02

Strategy & Quote

Phase 02

We build a customized operational blueprint suited to the specific parameters of your target. Transparent flat-fee proposals ensure zero unexpected charges.

  • Actionable operational roadmap
  • Resource & field agent allocation
  • Clear, transparent pricing
03

Field Operations

Phase 03

Our experienced field operatives deploy discreetly using high-tech equipment to observe, document, and extract critical intelligence without alerting targets.

  • Covert surveillance & tracking
  • Background sweeps & asset verification
  • Real-time secure client progress updates
04

Evidence & Reporting

Phase 04

All gathered intelligence is organized into a detailed, court-admissible report complete with timestamps, photos, videos, and professional analysis.

  • Comprehensive digital dossier
  • High-definition media proof
  • Secure, encrypted file transfer
